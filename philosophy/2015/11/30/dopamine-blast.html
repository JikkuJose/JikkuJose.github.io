Perpetual Dopamine Blast

Would like to present a thought experiment:

Imagine if you can do a brain alteration (be it non invasive) to change your preferences in such a way that you will fall deeply/unwaveringly in love with someone. Without fatigue.

And this becomes clinical proven to be okay and also reversible at disposable cost.

Will you consider doing this so that whatever be the state of your mind right now, after this you will have the bliss of honeymoon phase for the rest of your life with your spouse or girl friend.

If you are thinking of just trying it out temporarily, do you think you will be able to take the decision to undo it?
